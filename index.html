Why Trenchless Companies Win in AI Search

The AI Search Advantage for SLB's Category

AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google Gemini are especially useful for people researching technical services they do not fully understand. "What is CIPP pipe lining?" "How does trenchless pipe repair work?" "Who does municipal sewer rehabilitation in Indiana?" — these are exactly the questions where AI assistants provide detailed answers and recommend specific companies.

SLB is positioned to win in this environment because the services are technical, the content on the site explains the technology well, and the company name appears in association with the right queries. The two confirmed chatgpt.com calls prove this is already happening.

The recommendation is to accelerate this advantage with structured FAQ content on specific technical questions, schema markup, and content that directly answers the questions AI systems are being asked about trenchless pipe solutions.

Content to Create for AI Visibility

  • FAQ page: "How long does CIPP pipe lining last?" "What is the cost of trenchless pipe lining per foot?" "Can you line cast iron pipes?" — direct answers to what AI assistants are asked
  • Municipal services page with specific language: "city sewer rehabilitation," "municipal water main relining," "DOT-approved pipe lining" — terms appearing in procurement queries
  • Location + service pages: "pipe relining Indianapolis," "CIPP lining Bloomington Indiana," "trenchless pipe repair Fort Wayne" — captures both traditional and AI local queries
  • Add structured data (FAQPage schema) to existing technical pages so Google AI Overviews credit SLB explicitly

Track AI Performance Going Forward

  • The chatgpt.com source label in CallRail is invaluable — keep it active and add a disposition tag specifically for AI-sourced calls
  • Monitor GA4's "AI Assistant" channel monthly. It showed 2 sessions in June — this number should grow significantly in Q3 2026
  • Watch for Perplexity.ai, claude.ai, bing.com/chat as additional AI referral sources in CallRail. These will show up as callers find SLB through different AI platforms
  • Consider a short landing page specifically for AI-referred visitors: emphasize credentials, project portfolio, and contact form — AI-referred visitors tend to be further along in their decision
